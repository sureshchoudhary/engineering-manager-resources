# Great Engineering Manager Resources

Welcome to the Great Engineering Manager Resources! This repository aims to collect and curate a list of valuable resources for engineering managers, including books, articles, blogs, and notes, to help you excel in your role as a leader in the tech industry.

The target audience is beginner engineering managers and tech leads are looking to step into a management role. The resources are coming from the tons of notes taken by [Suresh Choudhary](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mrsureshchoudhary/) through his experience.
